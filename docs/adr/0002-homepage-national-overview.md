# Use a national overview on the homepage

**Status:** accepted

The homepage will present a National Overview instead of a user-selected named wholesale market, so its primary job is broad daily orientation rather than venue-specific monitoring. The homepage will remove the market-name display and market selector; named-market filtering remains available in search and other explicit comparison flows.

## Considered options

- Keep the single-market homepage and make the selector more prominent.
- Use a National Overview on the homepage while retaining market-specific controls in search and comparison flows — selected.

## Consequences

- Homepage copy, trend, rest-day, weather-risk, and links must no longer imply a selected market.
- Market-specific data remains available where the user has an explicit crop or market-comparison intent.
- The homepage needs a clear national-scope label so users do not mistake the aggregate for a physical market.
